ABOUT US

Welcome to E-Ligtas Mo Sys, an innovative Emergency Preparedness and Monitoring System developed for the Junior Philippine Computer Society (JPCS) at Batangas State University – The National Engineering University, Lipa Campus. Our mission is to enhance the safety and readiness of our university community through centralized access to vital disaster information and streamlined communication during emergencies.

  Through our platform, students can easily access emergency contacts, safety guidelines, and report their status directly to the Disaster Risk Reduction and Management (DRRM) team. This initiative fosters a quick and efficient response, empowering students to stay safe and resilient in times of crisis.

  Our team, known as The Innovators, is dedicated to ensuring that this system serves the needs of both students and the DRRM team, making safety management more accessible and effective. We believe in building a more prepared and informed community to handle any emergency situation.
