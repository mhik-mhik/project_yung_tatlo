1. Getting Started

a. Creating an Account

To create an account:

  1. Go to the login page.
  2. Click on "Create Account."
  3. Verify your sr-code. If you are a JPCS member, the registration form will appear. If not, account creation is not permitted, as the system is designed exclusively for the members of JPCS.
  4. Complete the required fields.
  5. Click "Submit" and then proceed to login.

b. Logging In

To log in:

  1. Go to the login page.
  2. Enter your registered sr-code and password.
  3. Click "Login." If you encounter issues, use the "Reset Password" option.

c. Resetting Your Password

If you forget your password:

  1. Click on "Forgot Password?" on the login page.
  2. Enter your old password and create a new one.
  3. If the passwords match, it will automatically update your password.

2. Using the System

a. Accessing Emergency Information

To access emergency guidelines and contacts:

  1. Log into your account.
  2. Navigate to the "Disasters" and "Emergency Hotlines" section from your dashboard.
  3. Review the safety guidelines and emergency contact list.

b. Reporting Your Safety Status

To report your safety status:

  1. Go to the "Report" section.
  2. If you have an emergency to report, complete the required form with details about the incident.
  3. Click "Submit" to send your report to the DRRM team.

c. Viewing Emergency Announcements

Emergency announcements will be displayed on your dashboard after logging in. Stay updated with the latest information from the DRRM team.

d. Managing Your Profile

To manage your profile:

  1. Click the “Profile” section.
  2. You can update your personal information and change your password.

3. Troubleshooting Common Issues

a. Login Problems

If you can't log in:

  • Ensure your sr-code and password are correct.
  • Use the "Reset Password" option if you've forgotten your credentials.

b. Reporting Issues

If you encounter issues while reporting your safety status:

  • Make sure all required fields are filled in.
  • If the form doesn’t submit, check your internet connection.

4. Contacting Support

a. If you need further assistance:

  • Reach out to the DRRM team through the contact information provided in the "Contact Us" section on your dashboard.
  • You can also send an email to jpcs-lipa@g.batstate-u.edu.ph with details about your issue.

We are here to help ensure you have a smooth experience using the E-Ligtas Mo System. Your safety and preparedness are our top priorities!
